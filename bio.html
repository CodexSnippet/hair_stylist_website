I am a graduate of Empire Beauty School. A professional stylist for 3 years. I have a true passion for the art of hair. I use hair as my canvas, bringing a distinctive aesthetic and fresh approach to draw out each client’s unique beauty. “Hair is my art and I love the process of transforming someone from start to finish.”

My mom was truly my inspiration. Watching and always eager to help her at her salon helped me understand at an early age that hair is not just an accessory; that great hair is the key to feeling beautiful inside and out. I knew then that I wanted a part in helping people feel beautiful. I then started my journey to mastering skills in cutting, coloring, styling and extensions. “I love hair and feel incredibly blessed that I’ve found a career that resonates with me so perfectly. As a stylist I believe in honesty, patience, creativity and hard work all functioning together to produce a beautiful end result for my clients.”
